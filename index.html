TicTacToe


 


     
Rules
  1. Player has to enter symbols.
  2. Symbols should not be a space or a empty character.
  3. Player has to click on start button to start the game.
  4. After clicking start they get a board to play.
  5. click on the cell to place their symbol.
  6. If the whole board is full and none of the players has won then "No one Won" message will be displayed, otherwise winning message will be displayed.
  7. Winning condition is "all cells in row or column or diagonal should have same symbol".
